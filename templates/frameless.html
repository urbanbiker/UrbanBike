Features & FAQs

What about Mobile App?

This web-site has fluid grid layout (not to be confused with "Responsive layout") and it easely could be used on your mobile phone or tablet. Resize your browser window to see the magic.

How long each "check-in" lives?

Public lifetime of each check-in is One Day, but we don't delete them, and your private check-in log should be always avaliable only for you. It's coming soon.

What does Login via Facebook do?

It requests you email and public info. We hate spam, and your email address is used only for associating your person in our system. We'll not post any messages on your wall.

Why it does not allow to login via Twitter?

We are working on that. Login via Twitter, Foursquare and Instagram is coming soon.
