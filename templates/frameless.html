Features & FAQs

How does it work?

The system just determines your physical location at the given moment and puts this info on map and onto the bike-tracking feed.

What info it leaves on the website?

It puts your name, picture and link to Facebook profile together with current time and phisical address. Yes, you need to log-in via Facebook to personalize your experience.

What does log-in via Facebook do?

It requests only you email address and public info. We also hate spam, and your email address will be used only for associating your person in our system. We don't post any messages on your wall and don't keep any passwords.

What about Mobile App?

This website is powered by adaptive design and it easily could be used on your mobile phone or tablet. Try resizing your browser window to see how it works.

Follow @urbanbikeme on Twitter. Any feedback and suggestions are welcome.
